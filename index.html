Our Methodology

We employed a systematic approach to develop and validate our crop prediction model.

1

Data Collection

Gathered comprehensive data on soil composition (N, P, K), climate factors (temperature, humidity, rainfall), and pH levels from various agricultural regions.

2

Data Preprocessing

Cleaned the dataset to remove inconsistencies, applied normalization techniques, and prepared the data for machine learning algorithms.

3

Model Development

Implemented a Random Forest Classifier, creating multiple decision trees to enhance prediction accuracy and reliability.

4

Training & Validation

Split the dataset into 70% training and 30% testing sets, evaluated performance using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score metrics.
